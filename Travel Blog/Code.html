Indian Road-trips & Hidden Gems

Short stories and simple guides for the modern Indian traveler.

Himachal Pradesh

Winter in Old Manali

Experience the magic of the Himalayas under a blanket of white. From cozy riverside cafes to the charm of wooden houses, here is why winter is the best time for slow travel.

Karnataka

Walking Among Giants

Step back in time at the ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire. Explore the boulder-strewn landscapes and intricate stone carvings that make this UNESCO site a surreal escape.

Kerala

The Backwater Slowdown

Trade the busy tourist circuits for the quiet life in Alleppey. Discover hidden canals, authentic home-cooked meals, and the peaceful rhythm of life on a traditional houseboat.

Kashmir

Paradise on Earth

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu and Kashmir, is a picturesque city known as "Paradise on Earth," located in the Kashmir Valley at 1,585 meters above sea level on the Jhelum River.

Assam

Where Tea Meets the Wild.

Rajasthan

Land of Kings

Udaipur, known as the "City of Lakes" and "Venice of the East," is a premier tourist destination in Rajasthan, India, renowned for its romantic setting, white marble palaces, and scenic, interconnected lakes.
